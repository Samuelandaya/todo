WHAT IS EARTHQAUKE?

An earthquake is the shaking of the surface of the Earth resulting from a sudden release of energy in the Earth's lithosphere that creates seismic waves.

CHANCESS HAPPENED IN EARTHQUAKE

The effects from earthquakes include ground shaking, surface faulting, ground failure, landslides and less commonly, tsunamis.
